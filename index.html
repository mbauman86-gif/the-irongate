02
The Four Pillars

Every detail of your estate. Handled completely.

I
The Inspection
Quarterly 50-point estate walkthrough. HVAC, roofing, exterior, irrigation, lighting, security systems, smoke and CO devices, dock and waterfront structures. Every system documented. Every concern surfaced before it becomes a problem.
II
The Maintenance
Grounds. Exterior. Interior cleaning. Tree and landscape care. Pressure washing. Painting. Fence and structure work. Marine construction. Coordinated, scheduled, and supervised — by us, not you.
III
The Network
A curated roster of vetted specialists — electrical, plumbing, HVAC, structural, marine, security. Every vendor personally selected and held to our standard. You never search for someone. We already know who to call.
IV
The Report
Annual capital improvement assessment delivered to you and your estate coordinator. What's been maintained, what's ahead, and what should be planned for. Your property as an asset — documented and protected.
03
How It Begins

From application to complete.

01
You apply — we review
Irongate accepts a limited number of properties each quarter. We review every application personally. If your property is a fit for our current availability, you'll hear from us within 48 hours.
02
The estate walkthrough
Your dedicated coordinator visits the property, conducts the initial assessment, and builds the complete estate profile. We learn every system, every nuance, every preference. This takes approximately three hours.
03
Your program is designed
Within five days, you receive a complete estate stewardship proposal — services, schedule, specialist assignments, and annual planning calendar. Nothing begins until you approve every detail.
04
Irongate takes the keys
From this point, your estate is our responsibility. You will receive monthly reports, quarterly inspection summaries, and you will hear from us before anything requires your attention — never after.
